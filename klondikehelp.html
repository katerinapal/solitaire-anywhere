Rules

Build up the four foundations from ace to king of the same suit to win the game. Aces can go in an empty foundation.

Build down on the tableau in alternating colors. For example, a nine of hearts (9 ?) can be placed on a ten of spades (10 ?). The top card in each pile may be moved to the foundations or to another tableau pile. A sequence of cards with alternating colors may be moved together to another tableau pile. Empty tableau spaces can only be filled with a king or a sequence starting with a king.

Click on the stock, the group of face-down cards, to flip over new cards to the waste. The top card in the waste may be played to the tableau or the foundations. Click on the empty spot to re-deal the stock. The stock may be re-dealt an unlimited number of times.

How to Play

To drag cards from the waste, the tableau, or the foundations click and hold while moving the mouse. To flip over new cards from the stock single-click on the top face-down card.

Scoring

10 points for moving a card to the foundations.
5 points for exposing a new card on the tableau.
5 points for playing a card from the waste.
-15 points for playing a card from the foundations to the tableau.
-10 to -40 points for re-dealing the stock.

Options

New Game - Click on the new game button or press the n key to give up and play a new game.
Undo - Click on the undo button or press the z key to undo the last move.
Deck Style - Chose your favorite card graphics.
Card Deal - Choose the amount of cards to flip from the stock to the waste. Check "show dealt cards" to view the buried cards.
Animate - Check the animate box to animate automatic moves.
Sound Off - Check this box to mute game sounds.

Tricks and Tips

Double-click on a card to have it automatically move to the best available location.
Press and hold numeric keys, K, Q, or J to highlight cards of that value.
Press and hold the shift key to highlight cards that can be moved.
Press the space bar to automatically move cards to the foundations.
